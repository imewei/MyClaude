# Paper Review

Systematic, structured review of a scientific paper.

## Review Template

### 1. Summary
- Main claim and contribution
- Key methods used
- Principal findings

### 2. Methodology Critique
- Is the experimental design appropriate for the hypothesis?
- Are controls adequate?
- Is sample size justified?
- Are methods reproducible from the description?

### 3. Statistical Rigor
- Are statistical tests appropriate for the data type?
- Are effect sizes reported (not just p-values)?
- Is multiple comparison correction applied where needed?
- Are confidence intervals provided?
- Are assumptions of tests validated?

### 4. Reproducibility Assessment
- Are datasets available or described sufficiently?
- Is code/software available?
- Are random seeds and hyperparameters specified?
- Can figures be regenerated from the data?

### 5. Key Findings & Limitations
- Do conclusions follow from the evidence?
- Are limitations acknowledged?
- Are alternative explanations discussed?
- Is the novelty claim justified?
